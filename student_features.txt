1. DANG NHAP & TAO TAI KHOAN
   - Dang ky / Dang nhap bang Email & Mat khau.
   - Dang nhap nhanh bang Google (OAuth).
   - Chon vai tro "Hoc sinh" (STUDENT) khi dang nhap lan dau.
   - Sau khi dang nhap, tu dong chuyen den trang Bai tap (/student/assignments).

------------------------------------------------------------

2. THAM GIA LOP HOC
   - Nhap ma lop (class_code) do giao vien cung cap de tham gia lop hoc.
   - Xem danh sach tat ca cac lop dang tham gia.

------------------------------------------------------------

3. BANG DIEU KHIEN (DASHBOARD)
   - Xem tong quan tien do hoc tap ca nhan.
   - Theo doi cac bai tap sap den han.
   - Xem diem so gan nhat va lich su hoat dong.

------------------------------------------------------------

4. LAM BAI TAP (QUIZ RUNNER)
   Hoc sinh duoc giao bai tap tu giao vien va lam bai truc tiep tren ung dung.
   Cac dang cau hoi bao gom:

   a) Trac nghiem (Multiple Choice)
      - Chon 1 dap an dung trong so nhieu lua chon (A, B, C, D...).

   b) Dien vao cho trong (Cloze Test)
      - Go tu/cum tu thich hop vao o trong trong cau.
      - Ho tro khong phan biet chu hoa/thuong.

   c) Noi cap (Matching)
      - Cham chon The A roi cham The B de noi cap dung.
      - Moi cap dung duoc to mau rieng de de phan biet.

   d) Dung / Sai (True / False)
      - Chon TRUE hoac FALSE (hoac Yes/No, Right/Wrong) cho moi cau.

   e) Sap xep cau (Reorder / Word Ordering)
      - Keo tha cac khoi tu de sap xep thanh cau dung.
      - Su dung thu vien keo tha (dnd-kit) than thien ca dien thoai lan may tinh.

   Tinh nang trong khi lam bai:
   - Dong ho dem nguoc (doi mau do khi con <= 5 phut).
   - Thanh tien do hien thi so cau da lam / tong so cau.
   - Ban do cau hoi (Question Map) de chuyen nhanh giua cac cau.
   - Nut Truoc / Tiep theo; cau cuoi doi thanh "Nop bai".
   - Tu dong luu dap an vao bo nho cuc bo (localStorage) de khong mat bai khi mat mang.

   Che do chong gian lan (Focus Mode - neu giao vien bat):
   - Canh bao khi roi khoi tab trinh duyet hoac thu nho ung dung.
   - Tu dong nop bai sau 3 lan vi pham.

------------------------------------------------------------

5. XEM KET QUA & DAP AN
   - Xem diem so sau khi nop bai (neu giao vien cho phep).
   - Xem chi tiet tung cau: dap an da chon, dap an dung (danh dau mau).
   - Xem loi giai thich cua giao vien cho tung cau.
   - Nhan email thong bao diem + nhan xet tu giao vien (neu giao vien gui).

------------------------------------------------------------

6. HOC QUA THE GHI NHO (FLASHCARDS)
   - Luyen tu vung va kien thuc qua he thong the ghi nho.
   - Lat the de xem nghia / dap an.
   - Hoc cung am thanh phat am (TTS - Text-to-Speech).
   - Co the on tap theo bo the cua cac bai hoc.

------------------------------------------------------------

7. TRO CHOI HOC TAP (LEARNING GAMES)
   Hoc sinh co the hoc thong qua cac tro choi tuong tac:

   a) Flashcard Quiz
      - Kiem tra nhanh kien thuc qua dang cau hoi the flashcard.

   b) Flashcard Match (Noi the)
      - Noi the tu vung voi nghia tuong ung.

   c) Flashcard Sentence Builder (Xay dung cau)
      - Sap xep cac the de tao thanh cau hoan chinh tu bo flashcard.

   d) Match Words (Ghep tu)
      - Tro choi ghep tu voi hinh anh hoac dinh nghia.

   e) Sentence Builder (Sap xep cau)
      - Keo tha cac tu de xay dung cau co nghia dung.

   f) Robot Chat (Luyen hoi thoai voi AI)
      - Tro chuyen voi tro ly AI (Robot) de luyen ky nang ngon ngu.
      - Phan hoi tuc thi va tuong tac nhu hoi thoai that.

------------------------------------------------------------

8. HOC THEO BAI HOC & BAI DOC (LESSONS & BOOKS)
   - Xem danh sach bai hoc duoc to chuc theo chu de.
   - Doc noi dung bai hoc, nghe audio di kem.
   - Truy cap thu vien sach / doc theo (Read-Along Books).
   - Hoc ket hop chu va hinh anh minh hoa sinh dong.

------------------------------------------------------------

9. THEO DOI TIEN DO HOC TAP (MY LEARNING)
   - Xem lich su cac bai tap da hoan thanh.
   - Theo doi diem so qua tung lan lam bai.
   - Xem bao cao tien do ca nhan theo thoi gian.

------------------------------------------------------------

10. ON TAP & DANH DAU (BOOKMARKS & MY REVIEWS)
    - Danh dau (bookmark) cac bai hoc / the tu vung yeu thich de on lai sau.
    - Xem lai danh sach nhung noi dung da danh dau.
    - On tap theo lich trinh ca nhan hoa.

------------------------------------------------------------

11. CAI DAT TAI KHOAN (SETTINGS)
    - Chinh sua thong tin ca nhan (ten, anh dai dien).
    - Thay doi mat khau.
    - Tuy chinh thong bao.

------------------------------------------------------------

12. HO SO CA NHAN (PROFILE)
    - Xem thong tin ca nhan va thanh tich hoc tap.
    - Theo doi huy hieu / cot moc da dat duoc (neu co).
